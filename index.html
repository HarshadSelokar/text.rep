Description VORTEX is a competitive Valorant gaming event aimed at highlighting the dynamic nature of esports through strategy, communication, and teamwork. The event fosters collaboration among gaming enthusiasts from various colleges, promoting competitive gaming as a professional career pathway.
Srages & Format
Format : An event will be in Hybrid mode.
VORTEX lies in its focus on strategic team-based gameplay within Valorant, emphasizing communication, teamwork, and creativity.
Prize Amount: up to Rs. 70,000/-
Date: 20th & 21th January
Team: 4-5 Members
Entry Fees:Rs. 500 per team

  • Day 1 (Qualifiers): All single-elimination matches take place, with the final 5 teams qualifying for Day 2.

  • Day 2 (Finals): Round 2 matches and Finals determine the winners.
FAQs: 1. How to register? Visit our website. 2. How many people can there be in one team? A maximum of 4 members.
  • Participants can play either online from home or offline from the SVPCET campus.
  • Internet will be provided only on campus; players must bring their own hardware.
  • Team members can belong to different colleges, but all players must have valid college IDs.
  • Single-elimination matches. No overtime for draws; the winner of the 27th round advances.
  • The winning teams advance to the next round, and losing teams are eliminated.
